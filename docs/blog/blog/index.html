I've told myself I have to start first with waking up early, as that should be the easiest and mandatory step in this process. I've said that if I spend the next 10 years of my life reading about stocks, I should actually become half decent at trading. The goal is to make money in the game of capitalism. I don't want to work all of my life

His mood was a reaction to his perceptions, and he made a choice to influence the perceived reality of others by being a negative baby (he lost the game ha).

This is just a fun concept for me rn. Not to be taken literal.
